How DermiDew Works

1

Scan with Smart Hardware
Users begin by facing our setup, equipped with a high-resolution mirror, integrated camera, and light source. This is to capture real-time skin images under optimal lighting conditions.

2

Analyze with AI
Advanced machine learning models process the captured data to detect skin conditions like dryness, oiliness, blemishes, texture, and more.

3

Get Personalized Insights
The system translates raw data into easy-to-understand skin health metrics, trends, and alerts based on each user's unique profile.

4

Track Products and Routines
Through the DermiDew platform, users log and monitor their skincare products and routines, seeing what works and what doesn't over time.

5

Explore Smarter Recommendations
Users receive tailored product and ingredient suggestions backed by data and AI insights to ensure skincare that adapts to their evolving needs.
