About DermiDew

DermiDew is about reimagining skincare through smart, personalized technology. The setup features a mirror, camera, and light source that capture real-time skin data, analyzed by AI to deliver tailored insights to the user. Through the website, users can track products, receive personalized recommendations, and explore ingredients proven to work for their unique skin needs.
